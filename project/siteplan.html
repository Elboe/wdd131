Site Plan for Woolly Monkeys Website

Site Name

Name: Woolly Monkey Madness

Reason: The name "Woolly Monkey Madness" was chosen because it describes my passion for these primates and it's pretty catchy as well.

Site Purpose

The site is designed to educate and raise awareness about woolly monkeys, their habitats, behavior, conservation status, and the efforts needed to protect them.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: What are the major threats to woolly monkeys in the wild?
  • Scenario 2: How can I contribute to conservation efforts for woolly monkeys?

Color Schema

  • Dark Green (#2E8B57): Used for headings and navigation.
  • Light Beige (#F5F5DC): Used for the background.

Typography

  • Font 1: Arial: Used for body text to ensure readability.
  • Font 2: Georgia: Used for headings.
