About Woolly Monkeys

Woolly monkeys are fascinating primates native to the Amazon rainforests. They are known for their dense, woolly fur and playful nature. Discover their unique habits and learn how we can protect them.

Why Conservation Matters

These primates face significant threats due to deforestation and hunting. Conservation efforts are vital to ensuring their survival for future generations.
