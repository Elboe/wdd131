About Woolly Monkeys

Discover the fascinating world of woolly monkeys and why they matter.

Introduction to Woolly Monkeys

Woolly monkeys are a species of primates native to the rainforests of South America. Known for their playful nature and dense fur, they are an integral part of their ecosystem.

Habitat

These primates inhabit the lush rainforests of the Amazon Basin, thriving in areas with abundant trees and diverse flora. They play a crucial role in forest regeneration through seed dispersal.

Conservation Status

Woolly monkeys are under threat due to deforestation and hunting. Conservation efforts are essential to protect their habitats and ensure their survival.
